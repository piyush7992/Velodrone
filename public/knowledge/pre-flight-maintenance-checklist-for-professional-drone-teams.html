Pre-flight & Maintenance Checklist for Professional Drone Teams

2026-06-12 — VeloDrone Team

A consistent pre-flight routine prevents incidents and reduces downtime. Use this checklist before every commercial mission.

  • Inspect airframe and propellers for damage
  • Confirm battery health and charge cycles
  • Check firmware versions for avionics and payloads
  • Verify GPS/compass calibration and sensor health
  • Confirm payload mounting and secure cabling
  • Run a short hover test in a safe area

Maintenance cadence:

  • Daily: visual inspections and battery checks
  • Weekly: full systems test and log review
  • Monthly: firmware updates and detailed sensor calibration

Record outcomes in your maintenance log and escalate any anomalies to engineering.
