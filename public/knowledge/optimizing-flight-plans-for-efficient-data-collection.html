Optimizing Flight Plans for Efficient Data Collection

2026-06-15 — VeloDrone Team

Good planning reduces field time and increases usable data. These tips help teams optimize coverage, battery usage, and post-processing.

  1. Choose the right altitude and overlap for your sensor and GSD targets.
  2. Prioritize mission legs to avoid unnecessary transit time between sites.
  3. Use wind and sun-angle planning to reduce shadows and improve reflectance.
  4. Batch similar sites to minimize configuration changes on the ground.
  5. Always include ground control points for high-accuracy deliverables.

After flight: verify data integrity before leaving site; re-fly any missed strips immediately.
