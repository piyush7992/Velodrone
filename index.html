Defending India's Critical Assets with Indigenous Counter-Drone Technology

In an era where low-cost unmanned systems are widely available, protecting critical infrastructure, defence installations and public events requires a new generation of indigenous counter-drone solutions. Velodrone builds end-to-end systems that combine hardened sensors, AI-based classification, and safe mitigation options to neutralize unauthorised drones — all designed and manufactured in India to meet local operational and legal requirements.

Our approach is pragmatic: we design for layered detection, fusing radar, RF, visual and acoustic inputs to reduce false alarms and provide clear actionable intelligence to operators. That fusion is paired with secure communications and modular mitigations so customers can choose kinetic or non-kinetic responses that comply with regulations and operational constraints.

Product Families — Built for the Mission

We offer three product families tailored to different mission profiles. Survey drones deliver centimetre-grade mapping and rapid data processing for civil and commercial projects. Surveillance platforms extend situational awareness with long endurance and high-quality EO/IR sensors. Counter-Drone systems provide a layered sensor network and secure command-and-control to detect, classify and mitigate threats to defended assets.

Why Indigenous Matters

Sovereignty in defence technology reduces supply-chain risk and enables rapid customization to meet evolving requirements. Velodrone products are developed and supported locally, giving government and enterprise customers faster field support, access to source-level engineering, and the ability to integrate with existing security systems.

Core Capabilities

  • High-fidelity detection: Multi-sensor fusion for robust tracking even in congested RF environments.
  • AI classification: On-edge ML models that distinguish between authorised and unauthorised aerial systems, reducing false positives.
  • Modular mitigations: From geofencing and RF disruption to controlled capture or interception, tailored to the customer's rules-of-engagement.

Industry Use Cases

Our systems are deployed across a range of critical sectors. For defence and national security, counter-drone capability secures forward bases, logistics hubs and convoy movements. For critical infrastructure operators — airports, power stations, oil & gas facilities — layered detection provides early warning and gives operators time to respond. Event security teams use our surveillance and mitigation suites to protect attendees at large gatherings while minimising disruption.
