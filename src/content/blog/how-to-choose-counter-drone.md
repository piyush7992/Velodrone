Choosing the right counter-drone system depends on mission, detection range, allowed mitigations, and integration with existing security.

### Detection vs mitigation

Understand whether you need long-range early detection or short-range interception and what mitigation options are legally permitted.

### Integration

Choose systems that provide open APIs and standard outputs for your existing command-and-control and SOC tools.
